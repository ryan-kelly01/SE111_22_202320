Review

Ryan Kelly2/9/23

Lee, a member of the Shaolin Temple is asked by a man to go to a tournament that is being held on an island that belongs to man named Han, who is a former Shaolin monk, who went renegade and now is a drug lord. After being approched by some British men at a martial arts tournament Lee is asked to work with the men in taking down Han. Quickly on the Island Ham calls for the capturing of Lee by hid bodyguard, an American; in the proccess Lee leaves the man with a great scar on the face but ultimately the guard has killed Lee's sister who visited the Island with him.

Lee meets John and Kelly two of Americas strongest martial artists who join him in his task to take back the island. The two have a dark past that comes back to bite them and they are left alone and weak at the mercy of Han and his armies.
